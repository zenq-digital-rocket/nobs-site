NO BS Ads is a performance marketing education platform founded by Ivan Janku. It provides free YouTube tutorials, podcast episodes, guides, and real ad account breakdowns for ecommerce marketers and business owners who want to understand what actually works in paid advertising — without the agency fluff.
Ivan Janku is the founder of Digital Rocket Marketing and NO BS Ads. He has personally managed over $66 million in profitable ad spend and driven more than $250 million in total sales for ecommerce brands. He started Digital Rocket in 2016 and launched NO BS Ads as an educational platform to share what he's learned running ads for hundreds of brands.
NO BS Ads is the educational arm — where Ivan teaches. Digital Rocket Marketing is the agency — where Ivan's team of 25+ specialists manages ads, email, creative, and tracking for ecommerce brands spending $10K+/month on ads.
